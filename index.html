Franchise or Representative

Emoh Deco wants to cover as much of China as possible, that means geographical area and market segments.
We are looking for partners, that can be in 2 forms:

1. Open a Franchise store

Our store is a concept to make the visitors have an experience. The store lets visitors feel the atmosphere, touch the quality, gain the trust and want to have the same at their home.
The concept and the touch and feel of each store needs to be the same, anywhere anyplace.

To achieve that we not only have developed the matching design, we have a team ready to customize it to your space.
We also have training programs in place for the staff of the store, to guarantee not only the quality of service but also the product knowledge required by the demanding clients of today.

2. Become a representative

You can be a specialist in certain market segment, for ex. textile stores, florists, hotel projects, etc… or be located in a specific geographical area.
In both cases we’d like to discuss cooperation with you to become our partner in the form of an independent agent or local representative.
